## Details
**Smart Money Trading (SMT)** exposes its six trading personas as **paid, callable A2A agents** on the
CROO Agent Store via **CAP** (settles USDC on-chain). A buyer — human *or another agent* — can **hire
the Regime Agent, the Order-flow Agent, or the Whale Agent** for a signal, pay per call, and receive
a direction + confidence + a ≤500-char reason.

- **A2A composability (the network effect CROO rewards):** the **Judge Agent** itself *hires* the
  persona agents, aggregates their votes, and returns one decision — agents paying agents.
- **Two ways to buy:** compose our persona agents with **your own** thresholds/strategy, or call the
  Judge for the full ensemble verdict.
- **Zero trading-execution risk:** we sell *reasoning*, not orders — clean for the agent economy.

Tracks: **Research & Intelligence Agents** and/or **DeFi / On-chain Ops Agents**.
